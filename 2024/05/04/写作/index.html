占比句型

1）研究对象做主语
部分 ==accounted for== xxx% of 整体
The electricity produced by coal accounted for 47% of the total production in US.
Constitute, make up, represent, take up, comprise
2)百分比做主语
==The percentage of== 部分 is xxx% in 整体
The percentage of the electricity produced by coal was 47%in the whole production in US.
Proportion of, portion of
3）具体数据做主语
百分比+of +整体+be x% (of 整体) +==be accounted for by== + 部分
47% of the total electric energy productior was coal.
47% of the total electric energy productiorin US was accounted for by that produced by coal.
Be constituted by, be made up of, be represented by
4)用括号作补充引出数据
Most of electric energy productiol in US was by coal (47%)

3)outnumber 超过
Women with a postgraduate diploma outnumber their male ==counterparts==, 60% and 40% respectively.

动态图补充

定点数据

1)变量+ 特点(顶 or 底),at 具体数值
In 1979, the consumption of beef was the largest of these foods, ==at== about 225 grams per person per week.
2)变量+ 特点(顶 or 底),with 具体数值
In 1979,the consumption of beef was the largest amongthese foods, ==with== about 225 grams per person per week.
3)变量+特点(顶 or 底)，(具体数值)主要用于长句，避免描述多个数据而使句子过于冗长
In 1979, the consumption of lamb was similar to that of chicken (around 150 grams), but much less fish was consumed (just over 50 grams).

趋势句子

1)趋势句子+from+起点数值+to+终点数值+between起点时间 and 终点时间 /from+起点数值 in 起点时间 +to终点数值 in 终点时间
The figure for the older people in the USA increased ==from 9% to15%== between 1940 and 1980
An increase from ==10% to 27%== is predicted in the figure for the old in Japan between 2030 and 2040.
2)趋势句子+to+终点数值+in+终点时间
The figures for the Western countries grew ==to 14%== in around 1990
The year 2040 will see a significant increase ==to 27%== in the figurefor the old in Japan.
3)趋势句子(动词结构)+by+差值+起终点时间趋势句子(名词结构)+of+差值+起终点时间
The figure for the older people in the USA increased ==by 6%== between 1940 and 1980.
There will be ==an increase of 17%==(增加了17%) in the figure for the old in Japanbetween 2030 and 2040.

动态数据(现在分词)

1)趋势句子，reaching+终点数值+in+终点时间
The figures for the Western countries grew, ==reaching 14%== in around 1990.
the figures for the Western countries saw a growth, ==arriving at 14%== in around 1990.
==amounting to, standing at==
2.1)趋势句子(动词句型),showing+趋势名词 +of+差值
The figure for the older people in the USA increased between1940 and 1980, ==showing a rise of 6%==.
==experiencing, witnessing==替换showing
2.2)趋势句子(名词句型),v.+ing +by+差值
The figure for the older people in the USA saw a growthbetween 1940 and 1980, ==increasing by 6%.==
==只适用于变量做主语!!==

动态数据(定语从句)

1)趋势句子-时间段 when it/they reach+终点数值
The figures for the Western countries grew significantly between1940 and 1980 ==when they reached 14%==.
The figures for the Western countries ==saw a significant growth== between 1940 and 1990 when they reached 14%.
2.1)趋势句子-时间段, during which periodit/they+变化动词+by+差值
A significant growth occurred in the figures for the Western countries between 1940 and 1980, ==during which period they rose by 6%.==
